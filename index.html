Beliefs

There are two guiding principles that drive our ministries and efforts: the Great Commandment and the Great Commission.

The Great Commission

Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, 20teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age.

Matthew 28:19-20

We believe that one of the most important ways to obey the Great Commandment (to Love God and Love people) is by obeying the Great Commission and spreading the truth about salvation. If you have any questions about our beliefs, take a look at our Statement of Faith or feel free to contact us directly at any time to ask questions.

Four Pillars

While there are many facets to our growing church, we work to orient our ministries around these four foundational pillars. The pillars stem from our principles and keep our efforts focused and biblical.

Christ-Centered

We strive to keep Christ at the center of everything we do. We see that our love for Him is evidenced by how we love others.

Gospel-Focused

Walnut Creek Church wants all people to have a transformative, rich relationship with God.

Service-Minded

We desire to be a blessing to the greater Des Moines community and beyond. We work to serve individuals and organizations with thousands of volunteer hours and support.

A Family

We want to make disciples because Jesus said so. We work to duplicate our lives and see others raised up to even greater maturity and labor.
